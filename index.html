Arch. Fernando Aizpun M.Arch, MAAK(A)

Fernando has had extensive experience in Spain , where he undertook more than 20 projects, including the design of a city council cultural centre, restaurants and cafeterias.

He has also worked in Kenya for over 25 years , mostly in educational and residential projects, which have really sharpened his design skills.

One of his largest projects was the interior design of about 25 chapels, most of them demanding a high level of detailing and the use of high quality interior finishes. Fernando is a practicing architect with BORAQS, is a Strathmore Trustee and a member of the Strathmore University Council.

Arch. Mukasa Namulanda B. Arch (Hons) MAAK, BORAQS

Mukasa holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from University of Nairobi.

He has a diverse range of experience from his first 5 years of practice - first in Tectura International and later in Symbion, two notable architectural firms in Kenya.

This was followed by 9 years as a managing partner of Archten Architects. He now has over 14 years of experience as an Architect, having worked on projects ranging from residential schemes to commercial developments.

What we do

Services

Masterplanning

Urban Design

QuestArch offers successful regeneration and creation of sustainable places demands subtle cultural, environmental, and economic responses. Our urban designers partner with inventive and experimental design professionals from diverse perspectives to create places unique to their settings. We constantly advance the development of new tools and push innovative thought for design and communication. Informed by our expertise and experience, we craft thoughtful, multi-layered urban designs.

Interior Design

Architecture

Architecture is the cornerstone of QuestArch. Our expertise is across various building types from town houses, apartments blocks, educational institutions, corporate & commercial developement. QuestArch's expertise is not only limited to conventional architecture but also Industrial developments such steel fabricated factories and associated ammenities. Our Interior Design portfolio is diverse and offers our clients creative, efficient spatial design and creates a lasting impact to the users of the spaces.

Management

Project

QuestArch offers project management services for various developments. We assemble the team of construction professionals tailored to meet the expectations of the project. From project inception: acquiring the neccessary permits in tandem with local regulations. Design review: a critique of the design to incorporate best practices in the industry. Construction Management: to liase with the contractor & client to ensure the project is delivered at the right time, cost and quality.
